The story of Amancio Ortega
Amancio Ortega was born in Spain, concretly in village from Leon.
In 1950 he left the school and starts to work for a shirt maker. With
the pass of the years he founded Confecciones GOA and start to sell 
bathrobes.  8 years later, opened his first store of Zara with her wife.
and only 10 years later it will be the biggest fashion group in the world.
In 2001 the company starts to be public and in 2008 had 4000 stores in 70
countries. 3 years later he was retired ftom inditex and its going to be the 
most rich person in the world. Finally in 2017 donated 300millions
dollars to fight cancer in spain
